Welcome to my blog! My name is Robert and I've been working in web development for several years. I decided to start this blog to share my experiences and opinions about the world of web technologies.

What I'll Write About

I'll mainly focus on:

  • Frontend development - JavaScript, CSS, HTML and related technologies
  • Backend development - Node.js, databases, API design
  • Simple and pragmatic approaches to solving problems
  • Critical perspective on hype and unnecessarily complex solutions

Why Simplicity?

In today's world of JavaScript technologies, I often encounter excessive complexity. New frameworks, build tools, and abstractions emerge every day, but they don't always bring real value. I'd like to show that often less is enough, and simpler solutions can be much more effective.

What You Can Expect

I'll try to write practical articles with concrete code examples. I won't shy away from controversial topics - if I think some trend is overblown or unnecessary, I'll write about it openly.

My goal isn't to offend anyone, but rather to spark healthy discussion about how to do web development better, more efficiently, and with less complexity.

Looking forward to our journey through the world of web technologies!
